Who We Are

Founded on fit, finished with flair.

FAAF Collections & Souvenirs started as a small stall of denim and t-shirts off A.I.T. Road, Alagbado — and grew into a full wardrobe destination for men and women across Lagos.

Today we carry everything from everyday jeans and tees to statement Jalabia and Abayah, footwear, bags, sunglasses, and the party souvenirs that make every wedding and owambe memorable.

Whatever the occasion, our goal stays the same: help you walk out looking — and feeling — like yourself, only better.
